Feynman Writing Prompts - In a seprate text file that you create, write out explanations of the following concepts like you are explaining it to a 12 year old. Doing this will help you quickly discover any holes in your understanding. Ask your questions on Slack.

Variables- Variables are ways to store something for later use. .It contains a keyword, space, then the variable.
Strings- Strings are blocks of text defined by quotations.
Functions (arguments, return)- functions are how we compute our variables. Functions control what we do with the data.
if statements- if statements provide a fork in a function and allows us to go two separate ways. 
Boolean values (true, false)- Boolean statements are how we determine whether functions are true or false.
