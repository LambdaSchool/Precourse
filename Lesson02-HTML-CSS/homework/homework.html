It can be cooked plain or cooked with vegetables like carrots, green peas, green pepper and every other veggie in the same league. It can be eaten with moi moi,another local Nigerian dish ( this one is made out of beans). Finally, you know what they say yeah? Food tastes better with coke! My favorite Resturant lives here!
