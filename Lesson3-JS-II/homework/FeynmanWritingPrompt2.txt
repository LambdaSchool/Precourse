Feynman Writing Prompts - Write out explanations of the following concepts like 
you are explaining it to a 12 year old. 
Doing this will help you quickly discover any holes in your understanding. 
Ask your questions on Slack.

for - is a loop that evaluates expression over and over again until it either returns what
	we're looking for or stops.
	(ex. for (let i =0; i< 10; i++) | for (*declare variable*; *conditional expression*; *increment var*))

&&, ||, ! - (&& is AND), (|| is OR), (! is NOT)

Arrays - Arrays are like storage containers for collections of data. In order to build an array just set a variable to [],
	then add the data types you want to the array to then be accessed whenever you'd like.
