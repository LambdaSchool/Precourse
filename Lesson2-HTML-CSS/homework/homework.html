Eggcellent Eggs! What versatile and beautiful specimans they are! Chicken eggs, duck eggs, quail eggs... I love them on an english muffin with avocado and kimchi. When they are fried in a skillet with heaps of butter, they make any novice in the kitchen seem like a cook extraordinaire. If you've never had such a thing and you're around the Bay Area, please check out Sequoia Diner and get your egg on.
