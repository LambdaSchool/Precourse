Oh, to choose a favorite food! A food to choose above all others, what qualities could such a food have? The crisp, slight saltiness of a grilled cheese sandwhich. The rich, nuanced flavor of Pakistani kima. The sweet, overpowering mantra of marshmallows to be found in rocky road ice cream. No, truly no, a single favorite food can not be named, let alone described. We must settle for three. Gateway to India was my favorite restaurant when I lived in Tacoma. I miss it terribly.
