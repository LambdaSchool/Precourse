	* Closure
  Is a nesting concept where the inner most nesting (level) of data will 
	be able to see their data and all the data at higher levels. If a same 
	named variable is used, parsing will start at the inner most level 
	until a variable of the same name is found. If the variable is not 
	found at that nesting, it will look a level higher.
