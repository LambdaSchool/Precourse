	What are objects?
    Objects hold many information about one topic. This is expressed by using curly braces {} which holds values.
  
  What are properties?
    Properties is the values stated within the curly braces used to describe the information.
  
	What are methods?
    Functions saved on an object.

  What are for in loop?
    Declaration of variables within the braces in a loop.

	
	Dot notation vs bracket notation:
    Dot notation describes the method or value in an object and bracket notations acesses the loop to keep going until it is complete or it breaks.
